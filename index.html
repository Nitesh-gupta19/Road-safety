Road Safety in India

Accident Statistics

India has one of the highest road accident fatalities in the world.

  • 3,66,138 accidents reported
  • 1,31,714 lives lost
  • 3,48,279 injured

Major Causes

  • Two-wheelers account for 43.5% fatalities
  • 68% accidents occur in rural areas
  • High speed roads increase accidents
  • Poor pedestrian crossings

Impact on Society

Many victims are young working people which causes economic loss to the country.

Reducing road deaths by 50% can increase India's GDP significantly.

Road Safety Guideline

  • Obey all traffic signals.
  • Follow speed limits.
  • Do not drink and drive.
  • Wear seat belts.
  • Do not use mobile phones while driving.
  • Use indicators while changing lanes.
  • Stop before entering a road.
  • Cross only at zebra crossings.
  • Look left-right-left before crossing.
  • Always walk on sidewalks.
  • Do not run across the road.
  • Drive within speed limits.
  • Follow traffic signs.
  • Maintain your vehicle regularly.
  • Allow pedestrians to cross.
  • Avoid rash driving.
  • Always walk on the footpath.
  • Cross at traffic signals.
  • Never run across roads.
  • Follow instructions of elders.
  • Teach children road safety.
  • Ensure safe school transport.
  • Do not allow minors to drive.
  • Set a good example by following rules.
